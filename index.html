About me

Hi! I'm Natalia Andreola, a front-end developer based in Toronto.

My journey into coding started when I was in high school, but I ended up in business and marketing. My technical skillset combined with my experience working with developers, supporting clients and website management, pushes me to build applications that put users' needs at the core.

I am a continuous learner and I am never afraid to take on new challenges. I am glad to be coding again! With a strong collaborative mindset, I value knowledge sharing, and bringing new ideas to keep the team moving in the right direction.
